Hall Seat Project Management

Hall Seat Project Management is a Django-based web application tailored for university hostels. It efficiently handles room allocation, facilitates student complaints about their rooms, and provides tools for effective room management. Built with Django and Bootstrap, this project aims to simplify and enhance the management of university hostel facilities. As a self-initiated project, I developed it to address real-world hostel management challenges during my university years, leveraging Django’s backend power and Bootstrap’s responsive design.
